About us

The Industrial Research and Development (IRD) Department at Delhi Technological University (DTU) plays a pivotal role in bridging the gap between academic research and industrial applications. As a vital component of DTU, the IRD Department focuses on fostering innovation and facilitating the transfer of technology to industry for societal benefit. The department actively engages in various initiatives, such as the commercialization of intellectual property (IP) generated through cutting-edge research, collaborative partnerships, and industry-driven projects. Through its robust Technology Transfer mechanism, IRD ensures that the innovations and discoveries at DTU contribute to the growth and development of industries, aligning with the university’s mission of translating academic excellence into real-world applications.

A significant aspect of the IRD Department's operations is the management of Memoranda of Understanding (MoUs) with various national and international organizations, fostering collaborations aimed at addressing industrial challenges and creating a platform for knowledge exchange. By facilitating such partnerships, IRD strengthens DTU’s ties with the industry, promoting research projects that have a direct impact on the economy and society at large.

The department also spearheads the university’s efforts in Intellectual Property Rights (IPR) management. Through its comprehensive policy on IP, the IRD ensures that the innovations and creations by DTU’s researchers are properly protected and can be commercialized in a manner that benefits both the inventors and the university. This includes IP licensing, assignment, and the creation of spin-off companies based on university research. In addition, the IRD plays a crucial role in promoting Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) initiatives, ensuring that the university’s research and innovations serve not only industrial and economic goals but also contribute to sustainable societal development.

In essence, the IRD Department at DTU is committed to advancing technology, promoting research excellence, fostering meaningful industry collaborations, and ensuring that the knowledge generated at the university leads to positive, transformative outcomes for society. Through its multifaceted approach to technology transfer, IP management, and CSR, the IRD Department stands as a cornerstone in making DTU’s research an invaluable resource for industries, communities, and the nation.>
