Corporate Relations

At DTU, Corporate Relations stands as the cornerstone of our commitment to bridging the gap between academia and industry. We foster dynamic partnerships that fuel innovation, empower students, and drive impactful research. By collaborating with global corporations, government bodies, and pioneering enterprises, we create opportunities for knowledge exchange, skill enhancement, and technological advancement. Our dedicated Corporate Relations Office actively engages with industry leaders to curate internships, research collaborations, and Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) initiatives that align with societal needs. Together, we aim to build a future-ready ecosystem that benefits students, industry stakeholders, and society at large.
